Ship and Crew management

Email: crewmanagement@os-ma.net

OSMA Shipmanagement LLC provides Ship management and Crew management.

    OSMA Shipmanagement LLC provides Ship management and Crew management for different types of vessels, mostly for:
  • Oil and Chemical Tankers;
  • General Cargo;
  • Container Carriers;
  • VLGC and LPG vessels;
  • Fishing vessels;
  • Shallow Water vessels;
  • Bulkers.
Ship management includes, but not limited:
  • Technical aspects of ship operations;
  • Conformity with ISM, ISPS Code and MLC;
  • Ship inspections;
  • Overseeing maintenance and repairs;
  • Assistance during class and flag surveys and ISPS-related verifications;
  • Victuals management;
  • Dry docking and renovations overseeing;
  • Compliance to flag and port state requirements.

Crewing includes following aspects :
  • Initial interview, selection of crew with passed CES test of the latest version;
  • Ensuring proper qualification and certification of crew;
  • Crew training (both on board and ashore based on official Seagull Training system;
  • Endorsement per flag requirement;
  • Medical examination fitness certificates & appraisal reports;
  • Implementation and follow up of drug and alcohol policies;
  • Issuance of working gear;
  • Joining and repatriation (fly details booking, coordinating with agents, etc);
  • Visa arrangements;
  • Crew welfare.

Our Company can offer a wide array of services for shipping industry, including:
  • Follow up of shipyard repairs;
  • Vessel inspections for pre-purchasing, inspection and assistance in case of technical needs;
  • Catering Trainings for Cooks, Messmen and Stewardess;
  • Trainings of the English Language for Seafarers;
  • Deck Crew Training – Support Level.


We would like to propose our services to your good company and can supply your vessels with Qualified Crew, as we have sefararers database of up to 6000 applicants!
We hope for a fruitful future collaboration.
